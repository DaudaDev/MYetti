Our profile

The Masih Youth Education Trust (MYET) is a community development initiative founded by sons and daughters of the Masihiyawa community from the former Sokoto State (now comprising Kebbi, Sokoto and Zamfara States).

Our primary objective is to empower and contribute to the attainment of higher socio-economic state of the Masihiyawa communities in these States.

Our mission is to form a united, highly educated and prosperous group through support with education and social entrepreneurship.

Our vision is for a prosperous and self-actualised Masihiyawa community that stands as a beacon of light and a shining example of the realised potential of Hausa-Fulani Christians in northern Nigeria.

MYET is a registered body with the CAC.
